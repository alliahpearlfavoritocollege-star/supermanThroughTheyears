1978 • RICHARD DONNER

SUPERMAN: THE MOVIE

By portraying Superman as a representation of pure, unironic hope, Christopher Reeve set the standard. His primary distinctive trait was the excellent physical contrast between the dominating, effortless grace of the Man of Steel and the clumsy, insecure Clark Kent.
